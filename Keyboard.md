## view mode ##

| **~** | normal view mode |
|:------|:-----------------|
| **1** | view channel 1   |
| **2** | view channel 2   |
| **3** | view channel 3   |
| **4** | view channel 4   |

## histogram ##

| **F1** | histogram channel 1 |
|:-------|:--------------------|
| **F2** | histogram channel 2 |
| **F3** | histogram channel 3 |
| **F4** | histogram channel 4 |

## curves editor ##

| **SPACE** | add control points on curves |
|:----------|:-----------------------------|
| **X**     | delete last selected curve point |
